## Description — en

```
Open the app — your workout is ready. Dredfit is an adaptive bodyweight trainer for iPhone: no onboarding quiz, no equipment, no account. It starts easy and adjusts every next workout to how the last one actually went.

A THERMOSTAT, NOT A QUESTIONNAIRE
Other apps ask twenty questions and hope. Dredfit asks one — after the workout. Tough, on plan, or easy: one tap, and the next plan shifts. Want to be exact? Put in the number you actually did — a fact for a single exercise outweighs the overall rating, and from a standing start it sets your level immediately. The first workout calibrates the system.

ONE NUMBER PER MOVEMENT
Your progress is one small number per movement — ten of them. Each level encodes the variation, the reps and the sets at once: harder variations unlock automatically and start at a lower count, so the change lands softly. Three shortfalls in a row roll that one movement back a few steps — a breather, not a punishment.

MILESTONES, NOT MEDALS
A workout that unlocks a harder variation, crosses into more sets, or lands on a round number ends on one calm screen you can share. No confetti, no badges, no streaks to lose.

BREAKS ARE NORMAL
Two weeks away or more, and the plan offers to meet you a couple of steps lower — further down the longer the break. Nothing is lost: levels catch up quickly, and coming back is the only thing that matters.

WHAT'S INSIDE
• 40 exercises covered by a chair, a table, a wall, a towel and a step. The four pull-up variations stay off until you switch the bar on.
• A pull in every session for shoulder balance; the other patterns rotate evenly.
• Guided warm-up, hold timer, rest ring with countdown — all wall-clock based, so locking the phone loses nothing.
• Level chart, weekly summary, calendar, home-screen widget and a Live Activity for rest on the lock screen.
• Optional export of workouts to Apple Health. Full history backup as plain JSON.
• Plain-language technique: three steps and two common mistakes per exercise, in English and Russian.
• "How it works" in Settings: seven plain facts about the regulator — every number matches the engine.

ZERO DATA COLLECTED
No analytics, no ads, no third-party SDKs, no network requests at all. Your training history is stored on your device and never reaches us — that is not a promise, it is the architecture.

Free. iPhone, iOS 17+.
```

## TestFlight — What to Test (1.5.0)

**en**

```
• Fresh install: three onboarding cards, then straight to Workout 1. Skipping counts as seen.
• Rate the first workout with an exact number (expand the exercise row): the next plan should match your number, not the +1 step.
• Settings → How it works: seven sections — check the numbers against what the app actually does.
• A workout that tops out a variation (or workout 10) should end on the milestone screen; try Share from there.
• If the app sees a 14+ day gap: Today shows "Welcome back" once; either answer closes it for that break.
• Switch the device to Russian and to larger text sizes — nothing should truncate or overlap.
• If Health export is on, completed workouts should appear in Apple Health.
```

**ru**

```
• Чистая установка: три карточки онбординга, затем сразу Тренировка 1. Пропуск считается прочтением.
• Оцени первую тренировку точным числом (раскрой строку упражнения): следующий план должен совпасть с числом, а не с шагом +1.
• Настройки → «Как это работает»: семь разделов — сверь числа с тем, что приложение реально делает.
• Тренировка, добившая вариацию до потолка (или тренировка №10), должна закончиться экраном вех; попробуй «Поделиться».
• Если пауза 14+ дней: на «Сегодня» один раз появится «С возвращением»; любой ответ закрывает вопрос до следующего перерыва.
• Переключи устройство на русский и крупный текст — ничего не должно обрезаться или наезжать.
• Если включен экспорт в Здоровье — завершенные тренировки должны появляться в Apple Здоровье.
```
